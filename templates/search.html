How it works?

The search uses TF-IDF (Term Frequency-Inverse Document Frequency) to find articles similar to your query.

  • Results are sorted by similarity score (highest first)
  • Then by number of claps (most popular first)
  • Shows top 10 most similar articles

Make sure you have scraped some articles first!
